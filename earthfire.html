earthfire arts studio

This is a project website redesign for San Francisco local clay studio.

Project Overview

From this project, I learned a various aspects of small business. I ended up educate my client what is user experience design and how social media works, what is website structure and functionalities, and also spent a lot of time to discuss how we explore ourselves to expand business. Personally, it was hard to balance myself to meet our business goals and our passion.

One of big business goals is improve our sales of numbers, and the other hand, our passion is to provide earthy art hub to community. How should we hook up those two different things together and make profit? More directly, how can we make money out of arts? We are going to keep existing website as a base framework. The owner of this studio purchased template of this website and willing to keep it. The contents needs to be organized, some of them needs to be rewrite and replaced.

Summary

Goals

  • To recreate website focused on user-centered design and functionality
  • Provide information for regular and provisional customers.
  • Increase numbers of customers and getting people know about this studio.

Audience Profile

  • Main target audience is local San Francisco residences
  • Age range is 8 to 80 who cares about arts and creativity.

Tone

Earthy, crafty, relaxing, healing, natural, and homey.

Approach

Since this is about a small business, the owner of this studio was only one who I worked with. I and the owner spent a lot of time to talk about business ideas regarding this studio. Brainstormed with the owner and showed what is good options we should take in order to achieve our goals. Those are done in person meetings and some emails. I created competitive analysis, persona, sitemap, and did a brief presentation. My client was not familiar with UX terms, so I explained to the owner one by one until make sense. It was a good opportunity for me to practice to speak clearly and concisely. With client’s approval, I changed structure of this website.

Survey

Keypoints

  • Gathering feedback on the studio
  • Exploring the reasons people visit the studio and assesing their experience of that visit
  • What does he/she expect on visit

Participants

  • People who love arts
  • Parents who wants to provide creative activity to their kid(s)
  • The inner/outer sunset district neighbor

Survey Findings

Findings

  • Visitors age demographic ranges from 11 to 80 (+)
  • Most of visiters was reffered to this studio by friends
  • A few visiters saw this studio while driving/walking/taking bus
  • A few googled it
  • Visitors are interested in clay art and painting
  • Visitors like friendly and cozy atomosphere

My reccomendations are as follows

  • Keep and emphasize homey atomosphere
  • Visitors like family events (parents and kids, grandparents and kids)
  • Consider to start more class/workshop
