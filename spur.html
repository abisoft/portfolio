Summary

Total participants: 4

My app is pretty simple, online shopping app. Most of participants are familiar to use those apps. It was not too difficult to operate my prototype even though it’s very early stage of developing. Overall, it was successfully finished and I was able to get feedbacks. Participants were able to finish those tasks without problems. (Task1: shopping Task2: change password) But there are minor issues need to be solved. I need to change sizes of icons, position, and labels. ¬Each icons has to represent functionality, mostly I finished successfully but I will go back to refine them.

What they said:

  • First page should be look like more “homepage” rather than what I have.
  • The task1 (change password), very last page should have a button to go back to home screen. I have displayed message “Password changed successfully” and “checked” icon for now.
  • On shopping cart page, there should be “save for later” text message on each items.

What changes I will make on next iteration

  1. Homepage design I am going to change homepage design. It surprised me that most of participants are confused and frustrated on the first screen. It may be changed if there are displayed actual images (weekly promotion images).
  2. Label Need meaningful label. I tried first prototype as simple as possible, and I did. But first priority is to create this app easy to navigate. I will need more additional information to display.
  3. Sizing I will go back to my Skecth and need to make better sizing. Text, button, icons, and so on.
